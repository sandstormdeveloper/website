# 👋 Hey there, I'm Sandstorm!
Welcome to my page! I'm a game developer who enjoys making small games and pixel art. I’m especially drawn to minimalist, low-resolution styles inspired by retro games, and I try to bring that into most of my projects. I also like experimenting with different tools, from game engines and frameworks to programming languages and design software. 

Although I'm technically a software developer, I really enjoy the art and design side of game development. So here you’ll find both programming-focused projects like games, and more art-focused ones like assets you can use in your own projects :)
